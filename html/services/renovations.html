What We Remodel

Renovations That Bring Tired Pools Back to Life

Whether your pool is showing its age or you just want a fresh look, we handle every part of the remodel in-house — from the interior finish to the equipment that runs it.

01

Replastering

Resurface a worn, rough or stained pool with fresh plaster, quartz or pebble for a smooth, like-new interior.

02

Tile Replacement

Swap cracked or dated waterline tile for modern glass, stone or mosaic that completely changes the look.

03

Coping & Decking

Replace tired coping and resurface decking with travertine, pavers or modern broom-finish concrete.

04

Equipment Upgrades

Energy-efficient pumps, heaters, filters and salt systems — plus smart automation that cuts running costs.

05

LED Lighting

Color-changing LED lighting for safety, ambiance and a dramatic transformation after sunset.

06

Pool Expansion

Enlarge an existing pool or add a raised spa, tanning ledge or sun shelf without starting from scratch.
